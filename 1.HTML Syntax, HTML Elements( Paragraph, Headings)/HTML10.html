Introduction to Chemistry

Chemistry, the science that explores the properties, composition, and structure of substances (elements and compounds), the transformations they undergo, and the energy that is released or absorbed during these processes. Every material, whether naturally occurring or synthetically created, consists of one or more of the various elements identified in the periodic table. Although these elements are composed of even smaller particles, they represent the fundamental building blocks of chemical substances. There is no quantity of hydrogen, carbon, or nitrogen smaller than an atom of that substance. Chemistry, therefore, focuses not on subatomic particles but on the properties of atoms, their combinations, and how understanding these properties can be utilized for specific applications.
