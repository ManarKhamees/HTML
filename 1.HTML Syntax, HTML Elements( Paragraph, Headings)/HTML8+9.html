LINEAR EQUATIONS

An equation that has maximum degree one is known as Linear Equations. The graph of it is a straight line.

For example,

  • y=8x-2
  • ax1+ bx2-cx3=9

are some of the examples of a linear equaion in two and three variables.

Slope of intersection of two linear equations Linear equations

y1=mx1+ a and

y2=mx2+b

for the points P(x1, y1) and Q(x2, y2) is:

m=(y2-y1)/(x2-x1)
